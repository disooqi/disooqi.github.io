FARASA (Advanced Tools for Arabic) Farasa Demo
FROM: Dec, 2015   -   TO: Sep, 2019      http://farasa.qcri.org/

Farasa (which means "insight" in Arabic), is a fast and accurate text processing toolkit for Arabic text. Farasa can handle both Modern Standard Arabic or MSA ( the traditional de facto modus operandi for writing Arabic) and the different Arabic dialects. Farasa can perform word segmentation, lemmatization, Part-Of-Speech tagging, text Diacritization, Dependency and constituency Parsing, and spell checking and correction.

QCRI-MIT Live Arabic Dialect Identification System (or simply DialectID) DialectID Demo
FROM: October 2017   -   TO: September 2018      http://dialectid.qcri.org/

DialectID offers an identification system for live speech into five Arabic dialects namely; Modern Standard Arabic (MSA) - which is the de facto of the Arabic language, Egyptian dialect, Levantine dialect, Moroccan dialect, and Gulf dialect. DialectID is a collaboration between Qatar Computing Research Institute (QCRI) and MIT CSAIL.
