--- layout: default name: WISE title: WISE from: Feb, 2020 to: May, 2020 description: > WISE is a scalable natural language platform for querying RDF-based graphs. WISE proposes a novel three-phase strategy to transform a natural language question into a SPARQL query. WISE uses NLP and semantic affinity models trained independently of the targeted RDF graphs, i.e., there is no need for prior knowledge of the graph or redo the pre-processing. link: //cods.encs.concordia.ca/wise/ image: /assets/images/WISE.png ---
